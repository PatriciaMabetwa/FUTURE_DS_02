# Customer Churn & Retention Analysis
## Task 2 - Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

### 📌 Objective
Analyze customer data to understand why 
customers leave and what keeps them engaged.

### 🛠️ Tools Used
- Google Sheets (Data cleaning & Pivot Tables)
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualizations)

### 📊 Key Findings
- 26.5% of customers are churning
- Month-to-month contracts have 42.7% churn
- Fiber optic customers churn at 41.9%
- New customers (0-12 months) churn the most

### 💡 Recommendations
- Encourage annual contracts over month-to-month
- Investigate Fiber optic pricing issues
- Create retention offers for new customers
- Offer loyalty rewards beyond 12 months
